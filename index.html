MURAMI
Murami, derived from the Yoruba term meaning "Prepare me," encapsulates the essence of this predictive model. Specifically designed for renters in Lagos City, it offers an annual price estimate, granting valuable insights into the housing rental market's pricing trends. By utilizing this tool, renters can gain a clearer understanding of what to expect in the market.
